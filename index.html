Apple Cider Mimosas

Fall Cocktails

The weather is getting colder so it's time to transition from our favorite summer seltzers to something still bubbly but seasonally appropriate. These apple cider mimosas are the perfect cocktail for brunch, happy hour, or any time in between.

Ingredients

Makes 2 servings

  • 1 1/3 cup Apple Cider
  • 1 1/3 cup Sparkling Wine of your choice
  • 1/4 cup Orange Juice
  • 1 tablespoon White Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Cinnamon
  • 1 Apple (for garnish)
  • 2 Cinnamon Sticks (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Mix white sugar and cinnamon into a small, shallow bowl, set aside.
  2. Pour orange juice into a small, shallow bowl.
  3. Take wine glass (or glass of choice) and dip the rim into the orange juice about 1/2 inch and then immediately dip into sugar mixture.
  4. Pour 2/3 cup apple cider into rimmed glass, being careful not to disturb the sugar mixture.
  5. Pour 2/3 cup sparkling wine into rimmed glass, being careful not to disturb the sugar mixture.
  6. Stir liquids together a few times with a spoon.
  7. Cut apple into thin slices horizontally and de-seed as necessary. Cut 1 slit towards the center of the apple slice, being careful not to cut it in half.
  8. Garnish glass with apple slice and cinnamon stick.
  9. Repeat for second drink.
